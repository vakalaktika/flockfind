How the Flock score works

The score is a shopping aid, not a lab test. It combines three visible inputs and deliberately gives established reviews more weight than tiny samples. Missing price or rating evidence is never estimated.

46%Review evidence

Bayesian-adjusted rating plus a confidence bonus for review volume.

34%Quality signals

Material, clean removal, repositionability, washability, moisture rating, and specification clarity.

20%Price fit

Rewards useful coverage near the moderate-price sweet spot and penalizes large markups.

Good to know

“Verified” means price and coverage or product-level review evidence was visible when checked. Marketplace sample prices and seller-wide reviews are not used as product evidence.
