How the Flock score works

The score is a shopping aid, not a lab test. It combines three visible inputs and deliberately gives established reviews more weight than tiny samples.

46%Review evidence

Bayesian-adjusted rating plus a confidence bonus for review volume.

34%Quality signals

Material, clean removal, repositionability, washability, moisture rating, and specification clarity.

20%Price fit

Rewards useful coverage near the moderate-price sweet spot and penalizes large markups.

Good to know

Quality scores use claims visible in each listing. FlockFind does not receive retailer commissions in this prototype.
